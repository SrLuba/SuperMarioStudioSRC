Most of the sound effects were directly converted from this collection
made by LadiesMan217: https://www.smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=18816

All sounds have been converted to WAV/OGG using Sinc Function ~ 32khz
and manually editted one-by-one, a few of them were buggy or missing
(as mentioned in the link above) and I had to record them from emulator using
similar settings to keep it consistent. Sound effects from the Advance
series are also included.
